BLOTIC-College Chapter

Bharati Vidyapeeth College of Engineering (BVCOE),Pune



VISION

We strive to establish an All-India Community, furthermore, divided into the district, state, and national levels where the numerous college chapters would also come together and participate in different activities and competitions.
The organization focuses to develop a DAO for outsourcing work with skilled personnel from the community. This will help different projects to shift from Web2 to Web3 and help in the execution of the projects coming from our own Community.



MISSION

BLOTIC aims to create an inclusive community of web3 enthusiasts in India through a blended online and offline approach. BLOTIC collaborates with famous WEB3 instructors to provide knowledge from experiences industry experts, giving more refined quality knowledge to the future pillars. It helps in networking with numerous WEB3 based companies and start-ups to collaborate and provide internships to keep the bar high for keen learners. The team partners with different investors, helping projects to incubate and thereby reach the zenith.
